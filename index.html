What You're Doing: Rating Songs

• Rate each Beatles song from 1-5, 1 being the Top Tier of favorites.

• There are 213 songs; try to rate them all!

• If you are unfamiliar with a song, links are provided for Spotify and Apple Music.

• Each rating category holds about 40 songs.

You Like Me Too Much: Adjusting for Overflow

• If you rate more than 42 songs in a given category, the earliest rated songs will be "bumped down."

• If you rate more than 42 songs as a 5, the earliest rated songs will be "bumped up."

The Long and Winding Road Comes Together: The Results

• After rating at least 20 songs, check your results to have even more fun!

• Get stats about your Beatles tastes and preferences, curated playlists, and more!
